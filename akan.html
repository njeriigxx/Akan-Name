Behind the Akan Names

Ghanians are named according to the days of the week they were born.
Below is a list of both male and female names and their meanings.

  1. Dampo-Bodua or Akosua-Kwasi

    2. These names are given to children born on Sunday.
    They are believed to be naturally agile ,quick ,
    fast and highly-skilled while performing tasks.


  2. Badwo or Akoto-Okoto or Asera/ Adwoa-Kwado

    3. These names are given to children born on Monday.
    They are said to be peaceful.


  3. Kosia or Nimo- Ogyama/ Abena-Kwabena

    4. Children born on Tuesday are given these names.
    They are assummed to be friendly.


  4. Kwaku- Atobi, Bonsam/Akua or Daaku- Odaakuo, Obirisuon or Obisi

    5. These names is given to children born on Wednesday.
    Their appelletion is "Evil".They are assumed to be
    very stubborn.


  5. Yaw-Busuo-Operaba/ Yaa or Prek

    6. Children born on Thursdays are given these names.
    They are assummed to be brave. They are never afraid
    of any outcome.


  6. Kofi-Ntiful or Babune/ Afua, Kyini- BaafioorNkoso

    7. These names are given to children born on Friday.
    They are assumed to be naturally wanderers and are
    unable to be somewhere for too long.


  7. Kwame- Nyamewa or Adoma- Oteanankannuro/Ama, Atoapoma

    8. Children born on Saturday are given these names.
    The males are assummed to be ready for combat while
    the females are creative.
