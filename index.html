Behind the Akan Names

Ghanians are named according to the days of the week they were born.
Below is a list of both male and female names and their meanings.

Dampo-Bodua or Akosua-Kwasi

These names are given to children born on Sunday.
They are believed to be naturally agile ,quick ,
fast and highly-skilled while performing tasks.


Badwo or Akoto-Okoto or Asera/ Adwoa-Kwado

These names are given to children born on Monday.
They are said to be peaceful.

Kosia or Nimo- Ogyama/ Abena-Kwabena

Children born on Tuesday are given these names.
They are assummed to be friendly.

Kwaku- Atobi, Bonsam/Akua or Daaku- Odaakuo, Obirisuon or Obisi

These names is given to children born on Wednesday.
Their appelletion is "Evil".They are assumed to be
very stubborn.

Yaw-Busuo-Operaba/ Yaa or Prek

Children born on Thursdays are given these names.
They are assummed to be brave. They are never afraid
of any outcome.

Kofi-Ntiful or Babune/ Afua, Kyini- BaafioorNkoso

These names are given to children born on Friday.
They are assumed to be naturally wanderers and are
unable to be somewhere for too long.

Kwame- Nyamewa or Adoma- Oteanankannuro/Ama, Atoapoma

Children born on Saturday are given these names.
The males are assummed to be ready for combat while
the females are creative.
